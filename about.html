Responsive Web Design

Today people use many different devices to browse the internet whether for business or pleasure. Responsive web design (RWD) is the process of constructing your website so that no matter what screen size a visitor is using your website will respond and self adjust to fit that screen size. We adapted RWD early. Our group was one among the first responsive web design Chicago based teams.
